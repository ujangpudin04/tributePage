Ir. Soekarno

Founding Father of Indonesia

Here's a time line of Ir. Soekarno life:

  • Education

  • 1901 - Born in Blitar, East Java
  • 1907 - Soekarno get his Primary School or sekolah rakyat (SR) in Tulung Agung. he lives with his grandfather, Raden Hardjokromo
  • 1908 - Soekarno continued his education at Europesche Legore School (ELS), Mojokerto at 1913.
  • 1937 - After That, Soekarno continued his education at Hogere Burger School (HBS), Surabaya at 1916.In This periode Soekarno met with importab figure Sarekat Islam, that is H.O.S Tjokroaminoto. Even he has been lived in Tjokroaminoto's house.
  • 1921 - Soekarno finished his school at HBS. Then he continued his education at Technische hoge School (THS) and now most popular with Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB). Sukarno succesed get an engineer degree at 1926.

  • life struggle

  • 1926 -Soekarno establish Algeemene Studie Club in Bandung. This Organization will be a foundation of Partai Nasional Indonesia (PNI) which was founded by Sukarno on July 4, 1927,with the formulation of the teachings of Marhaenism.
  • 1931 -Beside his activities in PNI, The Dutch arrested him on December 29, 1929. Soekarno was then imprisoned in Sukamiskin, Bandung. He was only released on December 31, 1931.
  • 1933 - After his get freedom of prison, Soekarno joined Partindo (Partai Indonesia) which was a splinter from the PNI and led it. This resulted in him being re-arrested by the Dutch and exiled to Ende, Flores in 1933.
  • 1942 - Four years later he was transferred to Bengkulu. In Bengkulu Soekarno managed to escape and headed for Padang. Then crossed the Sunda Strait and returned to Jakarta in July 1942.
  • 1945 - Together with Mohammad Hatta, Soekarno proclaimed the independence of the Republic of Indonesia on August 17, 1945.
  • 1945 - Then in the PPKI session on August 18, 1945, Soekarno-Hatta was inaugurated as President of the Republic of Indonesia and the first Vice President of Indonesia.

  • End Of Life

  • 1970 -Soekarno's health began to decline since August 1965. After surviving for five years with his illness, Soekarno died at the Jakarta Army Hospital on June 21, 1970. Soekarno was buried at Wisma Yaso, Jakarta and buried in Blitar near the grave of his mother, Ida Ayu Nyoman Rai.
  • 1986 - President Suharto gave Soekarno the title of Hero of the Proclaimer on the commemoration of Heroes' Day in 1986 based on Presidential Decree 81/1986.
  • 2012 - It was only in 2012 based on Presidential Decree (Keppres) No. 83/TK/2012 that Soekarno was awarded the title of national hero. At the same time, the same title was also bestowed on Bung Hatta.
